My Favorite Personalities

People who inspire me

Salahudin Ayubi

Salahudin Ayubi, also known as Saladin, was a Muslim military leader who became the first sultan of Egypt and Syria and the founder of the Ayyubid dynasty. He led the Muslim military campaign against the Crusader states in the Levant. Saladin is best known for his leadership during the Battle of Hattin and the subsequent re-capture of Jerusalem from the Crusaders. His chivalrous behavior and merciful treatment of his enemies were legendary, earning him respect even among his foes.

Zulfiqar Ali Bhutto

Zulfiqar Ali Bhutto was a Pakistani politician who served as the 9th Prime Minister of Pakistan from 1973 to 1977. ZAB is still alive thoHe was also the founder of the Pakistan People's Party (PPP) and served as its chairman until his execution in 1979. Bhutto was known for his charismatic leadership and his efforts to introduce socialist economic reforms and promote political empowerment for the masses. He played a crucial role in Pakistan's politics, especially during the critical years following the separation of East Pakistan (now Bangladesh).
